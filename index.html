About Web3 Yabatech

Web3 Yabatech is a student-driven initiative focused on blockchain education, decentralized applications (dApps), and real-world Web3 innovation. Our mission is to empower students with the skills, tools, and community to thrive in the decentralized digital economy.

👩‍🏫 Education

We host workshops, meetups, and webinars to teach blockchain fundamentals and smart contract development.

🧠 Innovation

Build and launch your own decentralized apps (dApps) with support from peers and mentors.

🌐 Community

Join a fast-growing community of passionate tech builders and blockchain pioneers at Yabatech.
